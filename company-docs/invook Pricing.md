# Important Policies

**Credit Rollover:** Available on Pro and Team plans. Unused credits roll over for up to 5 months, after which they expire.

**Purchased Credits:** All credits purchased through top-ups are valid for 2 years from the date of purchase.

**Team Plan Minimum:** Team plan requires a minimum of 5 team members to activate.

**Credit Management:** Advanced credit management features are exclusive to the Team plan.

**Shared Credit Pool:** Team plan members draw from a unified credit pool, enabling flexible resource allocation.

**Annual Billing:** Save 20% on all paid plans by choosing annual billing instead of monthly.
