Invook uses a **credit-based system** for AI generation. Choose the plan that fits your creative needs. All plans include access to Invook's Drive and Canvas features.

<Info>
  **Annual billing saves 20%** on all paid plans compared to monthly billing.
</Info>

## Important Policies

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Credit Rollover" icon="rotate">
    Available on **Pro** and **Team** plans. Unused credits roll over for up to **5 months**, after which they expire.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Purchased Credits" icon="coins">
    All credits purchased through top-ups are valid for **2 years** from the date of purchase.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Team Plan Minimum" icon="users">
    The Team plan requires a minimum of **5 team members** to activate.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Annual Billing Discount" icon="tag">
    Save **20%** on all paid plans by choosing annual billing over monthly.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Free Plan Credits" icon="gift">
    Free plan credits **do not roll over**. 300 credits/month — no exceptions.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
