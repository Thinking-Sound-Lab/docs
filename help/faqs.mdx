  <Accordion title="Can I use AI tools for free?" icon="gift">
    **Yes.** Free users can access all AI tools on the Canvas to generate assets.

    **Free Plan includes:**
    - **300 credits/month**
    - Up to **2 Canvases**
    - Full access to AI tools

    <Warning>
      Credits do **not** roll over. Unused credits at the end of the month are not carried forward. **300 credits/month. No rolling credits.**
    </Warning>

    See [Billing & Subscriptions](/help/billing) for more information on plans.
